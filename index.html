My Work

"LOKI" Travel App

The ideal travel app designed to address the problem faced by travel enthusiasts who own pets. The app aims to alleviate the stress and concerns associated with planning trips while ensuring the well-being of their pets. LOKI provides pet owners with a comprehensive solution, offering features and resources to enhance their travel experience, whether it's a domestic or international trip.

Nonprofit Website Redesign

Rescue Dogs Rock's website is a non-profit organization in New York City. The current website lacked clear messaging and user-friendly navigation, resulting in reduced adoptions and donations. The solution we found involved a comprehensive redesign to highlight the organization's mission, streamline the donation process, simplify navigation, and improve branding consistency. The project aims to increase adoptions and donations by creating a visually appealing and user-friendly website that effectively communicates Rescue Dogs Rock's purpose and encourages user engagement.

"Nomad" Global Learning App

This user-friendly global learning app was designed to address the challenge of learning about different cultures without physically traveling. The app offers a solution by providing users with the opportunity to explore popular cuisines, music, and landmarks from around the world. With Nomad, individuals can easily access and learn about various cultures, immersing themselves in a virtual global experience right from the convenience of their mobile device.
